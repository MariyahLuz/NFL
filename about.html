About Us

Welcome to Nakimuli Foundation

OUR OBJECTIVES

• To mobilize, coordinate and formalize youth, women and fisher folk working groups as the pivotal centres for the implementation of the Organization’s programmes throughout the beneficial communities in all the 84 Ssese Islands in Kalangala district.
• To create and lobby from a productive synergy with the local government leaders from all levels of administration.
• To train over 200 youth and women groups in programmes and activities that will supplement and improve on their livelihoods hence achieving better standards of living for those beneficiaries and for the communities where they come from.
• To lend a helping hand for the vulnerable members of Ssese community (orphans, widows, persons living with HIV, Persons with Disabilities, fisher folk) with tangibles or services including scholarships, scholastic materials, boats and nets, medical supplies, food, etc to all those individuals that cannot afford to have such services.
• To boost the availability of financial services in the district in form of availing more affordable credit facilities to the people and encourage their savings culture as well as investment (SACCOS, Village Savings and Loans Associations (VSLASs) and hence a Fisher Folk’s Bank (Bank Y’Abavubi)).
• To promote environmental sustainability of Kalangala district by engaging the communities in the programmes, campaigns and activities that ensure environmental protection, conservation, and economic empowerment (promoting green economy).
• To boost the agricultural sector in the district with a greater focus on the oil palm out growers, micro gardening (back yard gardening) and climate smart agricultural technologies.

NAKIMULI FOUDATION

OUR VISION

To be the leading local organization in uplifting the standards of living of the people in all the 84 Ssese Islands that make up Kalangala district through ensuring community access to better social, economic, health and environmental services which will lead to sustainable development.

OUR MISSION

To empower and promote the people of Kalangala district especially the women, youth and the vulnerable persons through special programmes that will transform their minds and enhance their capacities towards living sustainable lifestyles in their various communities in the Islands.

EXPECTED OUTCOMES

• Formation and formalization of working groups (women, youths, and the fisher communities). These will be per Island and for Islands with bigger populations such as Buggala, more than one but not beyond 25 groups will be formed (per category i.e., women, youths, and the fisher folk).
• A holistic stakeholders mapping shall be done. Consultations and engagement meetings will be carried out with all the stakeholders regularly as the work plans for each programme will stipulate.
• Call centres will be established with ambassadors to Nakimuli Foundation selected in each Island.
• Awareness seminars will be organized for all the selected groups and communities, and these will be in line with the assumed specific project actions or activities that will be implemented under each programme.
• Trainings will be made to the beneficiaries on all hands-on programmes or activities.
• Supplies and/or donations of tangible materials such as medical equipment like scanners, ambulances and other medical supplies given out to different health centres in the district.
• Structures established such as construction of a hospital in Kalangala district.
•Amenities promoted especially under the tourism sector (constructing monuments etc).
• SACCOS and Village Savings and Loans Associations (VSLASs)) will be established and promoted in the district.
• Child sponsoring and provision of scholastic materials to the vulnerable students etc.
• Procurement of a fish pelleting machine.
• A well-constructed, equipped and fully functional technical institute.
• A renovated Kalangala district stadium with modern facilities on it.
