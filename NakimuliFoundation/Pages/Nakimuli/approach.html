Our Approaches/ Causes/ Programmes

Nakimuli Foundation

• Nakimuli Foundation seeks to change the lives of the people of Kalangala district and their environment.
• The Foundation intends to achieve this through the implementation of the 8 programmes and more as discussed in detail below.

Programmes include;

• Women Empowerment for a better Livelihood Programme(WELP).
• Youth Transformation Programme(YTP).
• Community Health and Sanitation Programme(CHSP).
• Environmental Sustainability Programme(ESP).
• Tourism Enhancement Programme(TEP).
• Financial Inclusion Programme(FIP).
• Support for Vulnerable Groups(SVG).
• Sustainable Agriculture Programme(SAP).
