Environmental Sustainability Programme

Nakimuli Foundation

ESP

Environment and conservation are key areas for development. We will address them as follows:

• Kalangala district community waste management program (waste collection, transportation, disposal, and treatment)
• Ssese community awareness on proper environmental management actions.
• Trainings in environmental sustainability programs (tree nursery establishment, management, and tree planting; construction of energy saving stoves; briquettes making; water resource management including water harvesting).
• Climate smart agricultural practices (turning waste into organic fertilizers).
• Alternative sources of energy for; lighting and cooking (biogas facilities in schools and some homes, simple solar lamps provided to some households).
• Communal Cleaning programs revitalized i.e., programs like *Tweyonje* of KCCA adopted especially at the landing sites.
• Eco businesses promoted in the Islands.
• M&E
